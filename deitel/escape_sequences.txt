| escape sequences | -----

backslach \ are called escape character -- it indicates that prinft is supposed to do something out of the ordinary.

when encoutering a backslash in a string, the compiler looks ahead at the next character and combines it with the backslash to form an escape sequence.

the escape sequence \n means newline. 

\n ---------> newline
\t ---------> horizontal tab
\a ---------> alert
\\ ---------> backslash. Insert a backslash character in a string
\" ---------> double quote
